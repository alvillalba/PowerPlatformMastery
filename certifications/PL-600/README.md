## üéØ Exam Tips

1. **Architecture focus**: This exam is about design and architecture, not implementation
2. **Real-world experience**: Requires significant real-world architecture experience
3. **Best practices**: Know Microsoft's recommended practices and patterns
4. **Trade-offs**: Understand trade-offs between different approaches
5. **Scenarios**: Be prepared for complex scenario-based questions

## üíº Required Experience

- **PL-400**: Microsoft Power Platform Developer (recommended)
- **PL-200**: Microsoft Power Platform Functional Consultant (recommended)
- **Architecture experience**: 3+ years designing enterprise solutions
- **Power Platform experience**: 2+ years with Power Platform
- **Leadership experience**: Leading Power Platform initiatives
